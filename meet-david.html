David Gabbay

Professional Magician, David Gabbay, has been practicing magic, and mastering his craft for over twenty years. David has performed for thousands across his career; from deep within the hidden halls of the Magic Castle, to delighting the open seas on Cruise Lines, to providing small, handcrafted magical moments that have remained with guests long after the event has ended. David is always ready to bring a bit a magic to any event, especially coporate events.

David Gabbay
